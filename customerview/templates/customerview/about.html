FAQ

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account or sign up if you're a new user.
  2. Browse through our selection of restaurants and choose your desired items.
  3. Add the items to your cart and proceed to checkout.
  4. Enter your delivery address and payment information.
  5. Review your order and confirm it.
  6. Once your order is confirmed, sit back and relax while we prepare and deliver your delicious meal!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also offer PayPal and cash on delivery as alternative payment options.

Is delivery available in my area?

We strive to provide delivery services to as many areas as possible. To check if delivery is available in your area, simply enter your address during checkout. If delivery is unavailable, you can always opt for pickup or explore other nearby restaurants.
